Who we are Pwy ydym ni

Programme delivery and innovation — idea to impact Cyflawni rhaglenni ac arloesi — o syniad i effaith

VDB (VandenberghUK Ltd) is an innovation consultancy working across the UK. Founded by Hannah Van Den Bergh in 2019, we've grown from a one-person consultancy to a team that has unlocked over £255 million for UK R&D projects.

VDB (VandenberghUK Ltd) yw ymgynghoriaeth arloesi sy'n gweithio ledled y DU. Wedi'i sefydlu gan Hannah Van Den Bergh yn 2019, rydym wedi tyfu o ymgynghoriaeth un person i dîm sydd wedi datgloi dros £255 miliwn ar gyfer prosiectau ymchwil a datblygu'r DU.

We work across the full innovation lifecycle — from validating an early-stage idea through primary research, to building the consortium and securing funding, to delivering the science and engineering that turns concepts into real-world outcomes.

Rydym yn gweithio ar draws cylch bywyd arloesi llawn — o ddilysu syniad cam cynnar trwy ymchwil gynradd, i adeiladu consortiwm a sicrhau cyllid, i gyflawni'r wyddoniaeth a'r peirianneg sy'n troi cysyniadau yn ganlyniadau byd go iawn.

Spin-out Cwmni deillio
Our first venture Ein menter gyntaf

NPK Recovery

In 2023, we created our first spin-out — NPK Recovery — to develop sustainable bio-fertiliser from human waste. With 8 scientists and engineers and over £2m in grant funding, NPK Recovery is proof that our model works: validate, develop, build.

Yn 2023, crëwyd ein cwmni deillio cyntaf — NPK Recovery — i ddatblygu bio-wrtaith cynaliadwy o wastraff dynol. Gydag 8 gwyddonydd a pheiriannydd a dros £2m o gyllid grant, mae NPK Recovery yn brawf bod ein model yn gweithio: dilysu, datblygu, adeiladu.

Working across the UK Yn gweithio ledled y DU

We work with partners across the UK — from universities and research institutes to government bodies and industry innovators — connecting world-class research with the funding and delivery expertise to turn ideas into impact.
