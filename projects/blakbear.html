BlakBear — Digital Freshness Sensors BlakBear — Synwyryddion Ffresni Digidol

Supporting the world's first digital freshness sensor company to reduce food waste at scale. Cefnogi cwmni synhwyrydd ffresni digidol cyntaf y byd i leihau gwastraff bwyd ar raddfa.

The Brief Y Briff

From lab to shelf O'r labordy i'r silff

BlakBear, an Imperial College spinout, invented the industry's first digital freshness sensor — a paper-based gas sensor placed inside food packaging that measures spoilage in real time, replacing static printed expiry dates with live freshness data. They needed support to grow from a university research project into a commercial operation deployed across major food manufacturers.

Dyfeisiodd BlakBear, cwmni deillio Coleg Imperial, synhwyrydd ffresni digidol cyntaf y diwydiant — synhwyrydd nwy ar bapur a osodir y tu mewn i becynnau bwyd sy'n mesur difetha mewn amser real, gan ddisodli dyddiadau terfyn wedi'u hargraffu'n statig gyda data ffresni byw. Roedd angen cefnogaeth arnynt i dyfu o brosiect ymchwil prifysgol i weithrediad masnachol a ddefnyddir ar draws gweithgynhyrchwyr bwyd mawr.
