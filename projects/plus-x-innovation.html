Plus X Innovation — UK Hardware Accelerator Plus X Innovation — Cyflymydd Caledwedd y DU

Designing the programme that launched 69 hardware startups and created 1,100 jobs. Dylunio'r rhaglen a lansiodd 69 o gwmnïau newydd caledwedd a chreu 1,100 o swyddi.

The Brief Y Briff

A national hardware network Rhwydwaith caledwedd cenedlaethol

Plus X Innovation was building a national network of innovation hubs to support hardware and product startups — an underserved segment that needs physical prototyping facilities, not just desk space. They needed help designing the accelerator programme that would sit at the heart of the Central Research Laboratory (CRL), originally on the former EMI R&D campus in Hayes, West London.

Roedd Plus X Innovation yn adeiladu rhwydwaith cenedlaethol o ganolfannau arloesi i gefnogi cwmnïau newydd caledwedd a chynnyrch — segment sydd heb ddigon o wasanaeth sydd angen cyfleusterau prototeipiau ffisegol, nid dim ond gofod desg. Roedd angen help arnynt i ddylunio'r rhaglen gyflymydd a fyddai wrth galon y Central Research Laboratory (CRL), yn wreiddiol ar gampws Ymchwil a Datblygu EMI gynt yn Hayes, Gorllewin Llundain.

What We Did Beth Wnaethom Ni

From programme design to scaling strategy O ddylunio rhaglenni i strategaeth graddio

01

Programme Design Dylunio Rhaglenni

Designed the 18-week CRL Accelerator structure and curriculum. Defined selection criteria, cohort size, and progression milestones. Created the support framework: mentorship, prototyping access, funding guidance. Dylunio strwythur a chwricwlwm Cyflymydd CRL 18 wythnos. Diffinio meini prawf dethol, maint carfan, a cherrig milltir cynnydd. Creu'r fframwaith cefnogi: mentora, mynediad i brototeipiau, arweiniad cyllido.

02

Innovation Ecosystem Mapping Mapio Ecosystem Arloesi

Identified the key partners, mentors, and institutional supporters. Mapped the hardware startup landscape and unmet needs. Benchmarked against international hardware accelerators. Nodi'r partneriaid allweddol, mentoriaid, a chefnogwyr sefydliadol. Mapio tirwedd cwmnïau newydd caledwedd ac anghenion heb eu diwallu. Meincnodi yn erbyn cyflymyddion caledwedd rhyngwladol.

03

Scaling Strategy Strategaeth Graddio

Informed the expansion strategy from Hayes to Brighton and Slough. Designed programme variants (BOOST for early-stage, Stage One Pre-Accelerator). Llywio'r strategaeth ehangu o Hayes i Brighton a Slough. Dylunio amrywiadau rhaglenni (BOOST ar gyfer cam cynnar, Cyn-Gyflymydd Cam Un).
